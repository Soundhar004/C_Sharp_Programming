Tribute Page: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Kalam, the Missile Man of India, was renowned for his pioneering work in aerospace engineering and his inspiring leadership as the 11th President of India.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV).
  • Led India's nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
  • Inspired millions through books like "Wings of Fire" and speeches.
